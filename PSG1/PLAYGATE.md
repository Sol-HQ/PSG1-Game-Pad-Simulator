# PlayGate Submission Guide

> **PlayGate** is Play Solana's official game publishing portal for the PSG1.
> Submit your game at: https://playgate.playsolana.com/

## What is PlayGate?

PlayGate is how developers get their games featured on the PSG1. Once approved,
your game is available to 10,000+ PSG1 owners across 15+ Solana communities.

## Submission Checklist

1. **Build the APK** — run `build-psg1.ps1` (Bubblewrap TWA wrapping your web game URL)
2. **App icon** — 512×512 PNG
3. **Cover image** — recommended 1200×600 (check PlayGate portal for latest requirements)
4. **Screenshots** — captured at PSG1 resolution (1240×1080) on the actual device or simulator
5. **Privacy policy URL** — required for Play Store / PlayGate submission
6. **Game description** — title, short description, long description
7. Sign in at https://playgate.playsolana.com/ and complete the submission form

## Notes

- The `twa-manifest.json` in this folder is pre-configured for PSG1 dimensions
- Test your game with PADSIM PSG1 (`?gp` overlay) before submitting
- PlayGate review is handled by the Play Solana team — allow time for review
